Why snowboarding is better than Skiing

Snowboarding is superior to skiing in many ways

Snowboarding Vs. Skiing has been a battle ever since snowboarding was invented. In the beginning, snowboarders werent even allowed lift tickets and would climb up the mountains themselves. But now snowboarders are all over America's slopes, no matter where you are. This has happened because of how truly awesome awesome snowboarding is. It is similar to other sports, like skateboarding, surfing, and other board sports. This makes snowboarding pretty easy to pick up for people who have experience in those other sports. Although some people dont like snowboarding because they think it may be harder, it actually is not that bad, and anyone can learn with enough practice and dedication.
